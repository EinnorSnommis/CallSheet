What to Know Going In

▼
Brand new program: Like Wilberforce, Dillard launched baseball in 2023. Still building = more opportunity for Aiden to earn a role from day one.
Same conference as Wilberforce: Both schools compete in the HBCUAC. If Aiden picks one, he'd play the other.
New Orleans: Dillard schedules midweek games against D1 programs (McNeese State, Southeastern, Southern U) — good exposure and competition level.
Coaching changes: The program has had coaching transitions since 2023. Melvin Marshall is listed as current head coach. Worth asking about staff stability and vision.
3+2 Georgia Tech Dual Degree: Biggest academic differentiator. 3 years at Dillard → 2 at Georgia Tech → TWO bachelor's degrees. Aiden's 3.732 GPA more than qualifies (needs 3.0, or 3.3 for high-demand engineering).
On-campus housing required: First-years must live on campus. 695 dorm capacity. Room & board bundled at ~$11,300/year.
Financial aid: 95% of students receive aid, avg ~$18,700. Athletic + academic scholarships available.

Dillard-Specific Questions to Ask

▼
About the Film & Fit
What stood out to you when you watched my film, and where do you see me fitting in with your program?
How many catchers do you have on the roster, and how many are returning?
The program is still new — what kind of player and teammate are you looking for to help set the standard?
About the Program
What's the vision for Dillard baseball over the next two to three years?
I saw you schedule midweek games against D1 programs — how does that level of competition prepare your players?
What does a typical week look like during the season — practice, travel, academics?
Can you tell me about the coaching staff and the culture you're building?
About the 3+2 Georgia Tech Path
I'm interested in Engineering — can you walk me through how the 3+2 dual degree with Georgia Tech works for a student-athlete?
Has any baseball player gone through the dual degree path, or would I be one of the first?
Would I play baseball during my 3 years at Dillard and then focus on engineering at Georgia Tech, or is there flexibility?
What pre-engineering courses would I take at Dillard to stay on track for the Georgia Tech transfer?
My GPA is 3.732 — does that put me in a strong position for the dual degree program?
About Money & Next Steps
What kind of athletic scholarship package is available, and can it stack with academic aid?
What does room and board run on top of tuition, and does athletic aid cover any of that?
With the tuition freeze for incoming cohorts, what would the realistic total cost look like over 3 years at Dillard?
Can we schedule a campus visit? What's the best time to come to New Orleans?
What's your timeline — when would you need a decision?
