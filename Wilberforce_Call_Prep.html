What to Know Going In

▼
Brand new program: Baseball was revived in 2023 after 80+ years — partnership with the Cincinnati Reds. Still being built = more opportunity for Aiden to contribute right away.
HBCUAC Conference: The only HBCU conference in the NAIA. Opponents include Dillard, Stillman, Tougaloo, Voorhees, Wiley, and others.
Historic 2026 season: The Bulldogs just competed in the HBCUAC Championship tournament — program is building momentum fast.
Home games: Played at Athletes in Action Sports Complex in Xenia, OH (off campus).
Engineering: Direct B.S. degrees — Civil, Electrical, Environmental, Computer Engineering. No transfer or dual-degree required. Major advantage.
Financial aid: NAIA schools can offer athletic scholarships. Academic scholarships also available. Nearly 95% of students receive some form of aid.
On-campus housing: Two residence halls — Henderson Honors Hall (men) and Living Learning Center (women). Meals included at Allen Commons dining hall.

Wilberforce-Specific Questions to Ask

▼
About the Film & Fit
What stood out to you when you watched my film, and where do you see me fitting in with your program?
How many catchers do you have on the roster right now, and how many are coming back?
With the program still being relatively new, what kind of player are you looking for to help build the culture?
About the Program
The program has grown fast since 2023 — what's the vision for where you want to be in the next two to three years?
What does a typical week look like for players during the season — practice, travel, film work?
How has the partnership with the Cincinnati Reds impacted player development?
Where do you play your home games, and is there a plan to bring games to campus?
About Engineering at Wilberforce
I'm interested in Engineering — how do athletes in demanding majors balance their course load with the season?
Are there engineering labs or classes that conflict with practice times, and how is that handled?
Do you have other players currently in the engineering program?
About Money & Next Steps
What kind of athletic scholarship package is available, and can it stack with academic scholarships?
What does room and board run on top of tuition, and does athletic aid cover any of that?
What does the realistic total out-of-pocket cost look like after all aid?
Can we schedule a campus visit? What's the best time to come out?
What's your timeline for filling the roster — when would you need a decision?
