What to Know Going In

▼
Inaugural season: This is the first baseball team in RCC history. They just played their first game in February 2026. Maximum ground-floor opportunity — Aiden could be part of building something from scratch.
NJCAA Division II: Unlike Roxbury (D3), Robeson is D2 — meaning athletic scholarships ARE available. This is a significant difference.
In-state tuition: At ~$2,400/year, this is the cheapest option by far. Even with off-campus housing in Lumberton (much cheaper than Boston or New Orleans), total cost would be well under any 4-year school.
No on-campus housing: Commuter school like Roxbury, but Lumberton is much more affordable than Boston. Still need to figure out housing logistics.
Close to home: Lumberton is only ~2 hours from Greensboro. Much closer than any other school in Aiden's mix. Family can easily attend games.
Region 10 competition: Plays against other NC and SC community colleges — Pitt CC, Rockingham CC, Surry CC, Louisburg, Wake Tech, and more.
2-year path: Like Roxbury, this is a 2-year school. Aiden would need a transfer plan to a 4-year school for engineering and to keep playing. Need to ask about transfer pipeline.
Engineering question: RCC offers associate degrees but may not have a specific engineering transfer track. Need to verify what STEM/pre-engineering options exist and which 4-year schools accept transfers.
Not yet postseason eligible: As a new NJCAA member (joined 2025-26), Robeson becomes postseason eligible in 2026-27 — Aiden's potential first year.

Robeson-Specific Questions to Ask

▼
About the Film & Fit
What stood out to you when you watched my film, and where do you see me fitting in with your program?
How many catchers do you currently have, and what are you looking for at the position next year?
This being year two of the program — what kind of player and leader are you looking for to help establish the culture?
About the Program
How did the inaugural season go? What did you learn, and what are you building toward for year two?
The team becomes postseason eligible in 2026-27 — what's the goal for that year?
What does a typical week look like during the season — practice, games, travel?
What are the facilities like at Pennington Athletic Complex?
How many players are on the roster, and how many are you bringing in for next year?
About Academics & Transfer
I'm interested in Engineering — does RCC offer a pre-engineering or STEM transfer track?
What 4-year schools do your students typically transfer to? Are there articulation agreements for STEM or engineering?
Do you have relationships with 4-year baseball programs that help players continue playing after their two years at RCC?
What academic support is available — tutoring, advising?
About Scholarships & Housing
As a NJCAA D2 program, what athletic scholarship money is available?
Since there's no on-campus housing, where do your out-of-town players live? Do you help them find housing?
What does the realistic total cost look like for a player including housing and meals?
About Next Steps
Can we come visit the campus and see the facilities?
What's your timeline for building next year's roster — when do you need a decision?
