What to Know Going In

▼
2-year school: Aiden would play two years here, earn an associate's, then transfer to a 4-year school for his bachelor's in Engineering. The transfer plan is the whole ballgame.
NJCAA Division III: D3 does NOT offer athletic scholarships. Aid comes through academic grants, need-based aid, and federal/state programs. 93% of students receive some form of aid.
No on-campus housing: RCC is a commuter school — no dorms. Aiden would need off-campus housing in Boston, which is one of the most expensive cities in the country. This is a major logistics and cost factor.
Out-of-state tuition: ~$11,100/year is affordable for tuition, but add Boston housing costs and the real total could be $20,000–25,000+/year.
Engineering transfer path: RCC offers an Associate's in Engineering. The MassTransfer STEM program can help transfer to UMass or other MA 4-year schools. Need to ask about specific agreements.
Distance from home: Boston is ~700 miles from Greensboro, NC. Factor in travel costs, support system, and being far from home.
Reggie Lewis Center: RCC's campus includes the Reggie Lewis Track & Athletic Center — solid facility infrastructure for a community college.

Roxbury-Specific Questions to Ask

▼
About the Film & Fit
What stood out to you when you watched my film, and where do you see me fitting in with your program?
How many catchers are on the roster right now, and how many are returning?
What does the path to playing time look like for an incoming freshman?
About the Program
What's the competitive schedule like — how many games do you play, and who are your main conference opponents?
What does a typical week look like during the season — practice, games, travel?
Where do you play your home games?
What's the culture and team size like? How many players on the roster?
About Academics & Transfer
I'm interested in Engineering — can you walk me through how the Associate's in Engineering works for a student-athlete?
What 4-year schools do your players typically transfer to after their two years?
Does the MassTransfer STEM program guarantee admission to UMass or other schools for engineering?
Do you have relationships with 4-year baseball programs that help your players continue playing after RCC?
What academic support is available — tutoring, advising, study hall?
About Housing & Logistics
Since there's no on-campus housing, where do your out-of-state players typically live?
Do you help incoming players find housing in the area?
What does the realistic total cost look like for an out-of-state player including housing, meals, and tuition?
What financial aid or grants are available for out-of-state students?
About Next Steps
Can we schedule a campus visit?
What's your timeline for building the roster — when do you need a decision?
